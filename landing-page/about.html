Our Story

Vyapar Sarthi was built with a simple mission: give Indian shop owners a smart, affordable, and easy-to-use business management tool that works in their own language. We saw that kirana stores, pharmacies, and small shops across India were still running on paper diaries, confusing software, or expensive ERP systems designed for large enterprises.

So we built Vyapar Sarthi — an all-in-one dashboard that handles billing, inventory, udhar tracking, and AI-powered analytics. No accounting background needed. Setup in 5 minutes.

About GBRO Industries

GBRO Industries is an AI, web development, and automation company based in Nashik, Maharashtra, India. We specialize in building modern digital solutions that simplify complex business problems — from intelligent AI-driven tools to full-stack web and mobile applications.

Our team combines deep expertise in software engineering, artificial intelligence, and user experience design to create products that are powerful yet approachable. We believe technology should serve people, not the other way around.

Why Vyapar Sarthi?

India has over 60 million small businesses, and most still lack access to affordable digital tools. Vyapar Sarthi bridges that gap — bringing enterprise-grade capabilities to the corner shop at a price that makes sense. We're proud to be built in India, for India.
