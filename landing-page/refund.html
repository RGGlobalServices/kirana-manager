Refund Policy

Our commitment to fair and transparent refunds

Effective Date: January 1, 2026

TL;DR: You can cancel within the 14-day free trial at no cost. After the trial, refunds are handled on a case-by-case basis for genuine issues like double payments, service problems, or technical failures.

1. Free Trial Period

All new users get a 14-day free trial. No credit card is required to start. You can cancel anytime during the trial period, and you will never be charged. We send a reminder 2 days before the trial ends so there are no surprises.

2. Paid Subscriptions

After the trial ends, your subscription converts to a paid plan. We offer monthly and annual billing options. You can cancel your subscription at any time from your account settings, but please read the refund eligibility below.

Monthly Plans

  • If you cancel within 7 days of the first paid billing date, you are eligible for a full refund
  • If you cancel after 7 days, the current month's fee is non-refundable, but you retain access until the end of the paid billing period
  • No partial refunds for mid-cycle cancellations

Annual Plans

  • If you cancel within 14 days of the first paid billing date, you are eligible for a full refund
  • After 14 days, refunds are calculated on a pro-rata basis for the unused portion, minus a 10% processing fee

3. Situations Eligible for Refund

We consider refunds on a case-by-case basis for:

  • Double Payment: If you were charged twice due to a technical error, we will refund the duplicate amount immediately
  • Wrong Amount Charged: If the amount charged differs from the plan price displayed at purchase
  • Service Not Available: If the Platform is unavailable in your region or does not function as described
  • Technical Failure: If a payment was successful but the account was not activated within 24 hours, and our support team is unable to resolve the issue
  • Duplicate Account: If you accidentally created multiple accounts and were charged for each

4. Situations Not Eligible for Refund

  • Change of mind after the refund eligibility period has expired
  • Non-usage of the Platform (we do not prorate for unused time)
  • Downgrading to a lower plan mid-cycle (the new rate applies at the next billing cycle)
  • Account termination due to violation of Terms of Service

5. How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, please submit a support ticket through our Support page with the following information:

  • Your registered email address
  • Transaction/Order ID from your payment receipt
  • Amount charged
  • Reason for the refund request

Alternatively, you can email us directly at gbroindustries@gmail.com with the same details.

6. Refund Processing Time

Once approved, refunds are processed within 5-7 business days. The amount will be credited back to the original payment method. Depending on your bank or payment provider, it may take 3-10 additional business days for the refund to appear in your account.

7. Cancellation Process

To cancel your subscription:

  • Log in to your Vyapar Sarthi account
  • Go to Settings → Subscription
  • Click "Cancel Subscription"
  • Follow the on-screen confirmation steps

Upon cancellation, you retain access to the Platform until the end of the current billing period. Your data will be retained for 30 days after cancellation, during which you can re-subscribe and restore access.
